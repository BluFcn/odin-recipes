Soup

A comforting soup perfect for any season.

Ingredients

  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Potatoes
  • Herbs (thyme, parsley)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Noodles or rice (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions, garlic, carrots, and celery. Saut√© until softened.
  3. Add diced potatoes and cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Add herbs, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat and simmer until vegetables are tender.
  6. If using noodles or rice, add them in the last 10 minutes of cooking.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed before serving.

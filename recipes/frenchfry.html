French Fries

Delicious homemade French fries, perfect as a side dish or snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes (Russet or Yukon Gold)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt
  • Pepper (optional)
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Paprika (optional)
  • Optional dipping sauces (ketchup, mayo, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips.
  2. Rinse the potato strips under cold water to remove excess starch.
  3. Soak the potato strips in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  4. Drain and pat the potatoes dry with a clean towel.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Fry the potato strips in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  7. Remove the fries from the oil and drain on paper towels. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika if desired.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

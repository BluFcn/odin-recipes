Pizza

Homemade pizza with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Add your choice of toppings and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top for extra flavor.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
